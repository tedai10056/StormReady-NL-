The NL Winter Reality

St. John's is one of the snowiest cities in Canada.

On March 8, 2024, St. John's set an all-time daily snowfall record of 53 cm in a single day. The 2024–2025 winter saw Paradise receive over 150 cm in a single week. This is not unusual — it is Newfoundland.
